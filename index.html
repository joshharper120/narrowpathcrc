Frequently

Asked Questions

What does it Cost?

Nothing — it’s free! If you’d like to support us, donations are welcome via e-transfer to finance@narrowpathcrc.ca.

Do I need to be a Christian to Join?

No. You’re welcome here. Come as you are — we’re building a supportive community for anyone in the region.

How fast do I have to be?

Any pace. Run, jog, walk, or volunteer — the goal is community and consistency, not performance.

How do I get involved?

Join a weekly run, volunteer, or sign up for updates. If you want to help lead, reach out through the newsletter form.
